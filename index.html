Common Questions

Answers to what new patients usually ask before their first visit.

Are you accepting new patients?

Yes — Wellspring Family Clinic is welcoming new patients of all ages. Book an appointment online and our care team will confirm within one business day.

Do you accept insurance?

We accept most major insurance plans. Mention your provider when you request an appointment and we'll confirm your coverage before your visit.

Can I walk in without an appointment?

Walk-ins are welcome for urgent concerns, though booking ahead means shorter wait times. Our 24/7 line is always open for anything that can't wait.

What should I bring to my first visit?

Bring a photo ID, your insurance card, and a list of any current medications. If you're a new patient, arriving 10 minutes early helps with paperwork.

Do you offer pediatric and family appointments together?

Yes — many families book same-day visits for multiple household members, including our pediatric team for children and teens.
